Step-by-Step Guide

How to set up your bot

Follow these steps after signing up. Most customers are live in under 15 minutes.

1
Register at jamiitek.com/chatbot/register
Create your account, choose a plan, and enter your business details. Your account is ready instantly.
2
Complete the Setup Wizard
Name your bot, describe your business, add your services and FAQs. The wizard is 6 simple steps.
3
Get your Meta WhatsApp Credentials
Go to developers.facebook.com → Create App → WhatsApp. Copy your Phone Number ID and API Token. It's free.
4
Paste credentials into your bot config
Enter your Phone Number ID and Token in the WhatsApp step of the wizard. Your webhook URL is auto-generated.
5
Deploy and test
Click Deploy. Send a WhatsApp message to your number. Your bot should reply within 2 seconds! 🎉
