04

JamiiBot & WhatsApp Data

JamiiBot operates on top of the WhatsApp Cloud API provided by Meta Platforms, Inc. This means WhatsApp messaging rules and Meta's data policies apply in addition to this privacy policy.

Meta & WhatsApp

  • Messages sent through WhatsApp are subject to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy and Meta's Privacy Policy.
  • Meta processes message metadata (delivery status, timestamps, phone numbers) on their own infrastructure.
  • JamiiTek registers business clients' WhatsApp numbers under our Meta developer account on their behalf.
  • JamiiTek only receives message content that Meta delivers to our webhook — we do not intercept end-to-end encrypted messages outside of the business messaging platform.

Groq AI Engine

Bot responses are generated by Groq API running the Llama 3.3 70B language model. When a customer sends a message, that message is transmitted to Groq for response generation.

Groq data handling: Groq processes messages in real time and does not retain conversation data after response generation, per their enterprise data processing terms. See Groq's Privacy Policy.

Business Client Responsibility

Business clients who use JamiiBot are responsible for:

  • Obtaining appropriate consent from their customers before collecting their data through the bot.
  • Informing their customers that conversations are handled by an AI system.
  • Complying with applicable data protection laws in their own jurisdiction.
  • Not configuring bots to collect sensitive personal data (health, financial, or identity information) without proper safeguards.

Hosting & Domain Services

When JamiiTek provides web hosting, email hosting, or domain registration services:

  • Website content — your website files, databases, and media are stored on our servers. You retain full ownership of all your content.
  • Email data — emails in hosted accounts are stored on our mail servers. We do not read or analyse email content.
  • Domain registration — registrant information is submitted to domain registries and may appear in public WHOIS databases as required by ICANN. Contact us to request WHOIS privacy protection.
  • Server logs — access logs recording IP addresses and request details are retained for 30 days for security and troubleshooting, then deleted.
  • Backups — we maintain regular backups of hosted data for disaster recovery. Backup data is encrypted at rest.
Your data is yours: JamiiTek does not claim ownership of any content, files, databases, or emails stored on our hosting infrastructure. Upon termination, we provide a data export window of 30 days.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data under any circumstances. We share data only in these limited cases:

You have the following rights regarding your personal data held by JamiiTek:

We will not charge a fee for exercising your rights. If a request is clearly unfounded or excessive, we may ask for a reasonable fee or decline, giving you written reasons.

Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organisational measures:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS 1.2+
  • API keys, tokens, and credentials are stored as encrypted environment variables — never in source code or version control
  • Production databases are not publicly accessible — all connections are restricted to the application server
  • Access to systems containing personal data is restricted to authorised JamiiTek personnel only
  • Regular security dependency updates and code reviews are performed
  • Hosting infrastructure benefits from DDoS protection and automated monitoring
  • Backups are encrypted at rest and stored separately from primary data

No system is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability in any JamiiTek service, please report it responsibly to security@jamiitek.co.tz before public disclosure. We will acknowledge reports within 48 hours.

Data breach notification: In the event of a data breach that affects your personal data, we will notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware of it, in accordance with applicable law.
