Part 1 · Getting Started

From Zero to Live — 6 Steps

1

Create a Website

Click ➕ New Website. Choose your business name, subdomain (your address — e.g. myshop becomes myshop.jamiitek.com), and website type. The type matters most: choose Travel & Tourism and the system automatically creates 5 pages plus Packages, Trips and Destinations sections. Choose Restaurant and you get a Menu; E-Commerce gets Products — every type has its own structure.

2

Fill in Your Business Details

On the Dashboard, go to ⚙️ Website Details: phone, WhatsApp, email, address, tagline and logo. These details appear automatically everywhere on your website — change your phone number once here and it updates on every page.

3

Add Your Content

In the menu (left on desktop, bottom on mobile) you'll see your content sections — e.g. 🦁 Tour Packages. Click ➕, fill the form (name, description, price, image...), save. Everything you add appears instantly on your website, wherever its block is placed.

4

Edit the Design (Editor)

Under 📄 Your Pages click ✏️ Edit Design. The editor opens — drag blocks from the side onto your page, click any text to change it, select any element to change colors and sizes. First-time users get a quick tour of the editor. Everything auto-saves.

5

Use the AI Assistant

Inside the editor, click ✨ AI Assistant. Describe what you need in English or Swahili — e.g. "create a Why Choose Us section with 3 reasons for a safari company" — then click Insert into Page. The AI knows your business name and type.

6

Publish 🚀

When you're happy, click 🚀 Publish on the Dashboard. Your website appears immediately at yourname.jamiitek.com. Before publishing, only you can see it — visitors see "Coming Soon".

Part 2 · For Your Type

Guide for Every Website Type

A

Add your Packages

Go to 🦁 Tour Packages → ➕ Package. Fill in: name (e.g. Serengeti Safari — 5 Days), description, price, duration, what's included (write one per line — it displays as a clean list), and the day-by-day itinerary. Upload a great photo — it's what attracts customers.

B

Add Trips and Destinations

Trips are dated departures (departure date, available seats, meeting point). Destinations are places you cover — region, best season, highlights. When a visitor clicks any package they get a full page with a Book via WhatsApp button — the message arrives with the package name already filled in!

C

Arrange your design

Your home page already has a hero, packages grid and destinations. In the editor you can move sections and swap images. The "JamiiTek — Your Content" blocks let you place the Packages grid on any page.

A

Add your products

🛍️ Products → ➕ Product: name, description, price, old price (for discounts), category, and stock. A clean photo with a good background matters a lot for a shop.

B

Orders via WhatsApp

Fill in your WhatsApp number under Website Details. Every product gets an Order via WhatsApp button — when a customer taps it, the message arrives with the product name ready.

A

Build your Menu

🍛 Menu Items → ➕ Dish: dish name, an appetizing description, price, and category (Breakfast, Lunch, Drinks...). A great food photo is an advert in itself.

B

Delivery via WhatsApp

Add your WhatsApp number and restaurant address. Customers order directly from every dish.

A

Services and Team

🛠️ Services: list every service with its description. 👤 Team: add staff, positions and photos. Edit the About page in the editor to tell your company's story.

B

Professional look

Upload your logo under Website Details — it appears in the navbar and footer. Choose the Side Navbar or Top nav style for a more corporate look (Dashboard → Website Appearance).

A

Promote events

🎉 Events → ➕ Event: name, description, date, venue, and ticket price. Hide past events by unchecking "Visible on website".

B

Featured events

Tick ⭐ Featured on your biggest events — they get a special badge so they stand out.

Part 3 · The Editor

Use the Editor Like a Pro

🧱 Blocks — drag and drop
The editor side panel holds blocks — sections, columns, text, images, video, buttons. Grab a block, drag it onto the page, drop it where you want. A line shows where it will land. On mobile: tap and hold briefly before dragging.
⭐ "JamiiTek — Your Content" blocks
These are blocks of YOUR content: Packages Grid, Menu Grid, My Contact Info etc. Place a Packages block anywhere and all your packages appear there — and when you add a new package tomorrow, it shows up automatically without touching the design. That's the magic of this system.
🎨 Changing colors, fonts and sizes
Click any element — the Styles panel opens. There you change colors, text size, spacing (padding/margin) and backgrounds. For your site-wide color, use the accent color under Dashboard → Appearance.
📱 Checking the mobile view
At the top of the editor are Desktop / Tablet / Mobile buttons. Tap Mobile to see exactly how your site looks on a phone — and fix anything. More than half of your visitors will be on mobile.
💻 Viewing the code (for experts)
Click the </> button to view your page's HTML and CSS in a modern code viewer. You don't need to know code — it's there for those who want extra control.
⌨️ Shortcuts
Ctrl + S — save · Ctrl + Z — undo · Ctrl + Shift + Z — redo · Delete — remove the selected element.
Part 4 · FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

When do my changes appear on the website?
Instantly. The system uses a fast cache, but it clears itself every time you save a change.
Can I build a website using only my phone?
Yes — the whole dashboard, adding content, images and settings work perfectly on mobile. The design editor works on phones too, though for longer designs we recommend a tablet or computer for comfort.
Where are my images stored?
They're uploaded to a global CDN (Uploadcare) — they load fast worldwide. Upload images up to 8MB; the system resizes them automatically.
I can't see my changes on the website?
Make sure (1) you clicked Publish, (2) you're checking the correct address yourname.jamiitek.com, (3) do a hard refresh — Ctrl+Shift+R.
Can I have more than one website?
Yes — click ➕ New Website. Each one gets its own subdomain, content and design.
